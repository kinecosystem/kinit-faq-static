Moving to a new device

ⓘ 05 Sep 18

Got a new device? Great for you! Remember to restore your account when you install the Kinit app (in the case you have already completed the backup flow in advance). If you didn’t back up your account on your previous device, you will not be able to get your balance back.

If your previous device is still available, we highly recommend you to backup your account so that you can restore it on your new device and continue using the app with your previous balance.
