How to backup your account

ⓘ 05 Sep 18

The backup functionality is available in the the app, under ‘More’. Backing up your wallet will allow you to recover your Kin if you get locked out of the app, or if your phone is ever lost or stolen.

The backup flow is as easy as setting new security questions and providing your email address. A QR-code is then sent to your email immediately.

Should you need to restore your account, use the QR-code together with the answers for your security questions.
