Can’t find the email with the QR code

ⓘ 05 Sep 18

The QR code is essential for restoring your Kin. If you did not save your QR code in the email sent to you, or in a secure place; please back up your account again now, if possible.

There is no way to restore your Kin without the QR code! We do not hold a copy of it, and you are the only one who holds it.
