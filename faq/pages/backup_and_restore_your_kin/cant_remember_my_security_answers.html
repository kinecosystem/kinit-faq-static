Can’t remember my security answers

ⓘ 05 Sep 18

Having trouble remembering the security answers you provided when backing up your Kin?
Remember the answers but the app keeps showing an error message?
Please notice the backup security answers are case sensitive. Hence, you have to write it in the exact same way you did when you backed up your account.
Also, in case you backed up your Kin a few times in the past, make sure you are using the latest QR code and security answers.
The security answers, as well as the QR code, are essential for restoring your Kin. If you do not remember the answers; please back up your account again, if possible. We do not hold your security answers, and you are the only one who can restore your Kin balance.
