Moving to a new device

ⓘ 05 Sep 18

If you completed an earn activity and cannot submit your answers, there are two possible reasons:

  1. Server issues. After a few minutes, you should resubmit your answers.
  2. Timezone issues. Try to submit again the next day.

If neither of these options worked, please reach out to us so we can investigate this further.

For iOS users, if you run into the issue of not being able to submit your answers on the very first task; please check that the Background App Refresh is set to ‘On’ in the general section of your phone’s settings.

Additionally, it is always helpful to check that your phone’s date and time match your relevant timezone.
